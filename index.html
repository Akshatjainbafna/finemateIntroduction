Finemate is a Software as a Service Startup. It provides a Retention Engine and a super app for all the Big, Medium and Small Size Institutes. It provides a student management system which includes Attendance Management system, Fees management system, Examination Management system, advance library management system, transport management system, Assignment management and a smart card which a student can use as a Identity card, Debit Card, Authentication card, Library card and Bus authorisation card all in one card see more...

We are going to provide a number of advance services through a single, simple and sophasticated application...

Retention Engine

Retention Engine aids you to retain whatever you learn from different sources on a daily basis which will help you to retain things you studied in 2nd semester or 11th standard in placements and throughout life

All-in-One Card

A single card that eliminates multiple cards and works as a Identity card, athentication and authorisation card, library card, debit card, Bus admit card and exam admit card.

Super App

A super app through which you can submit fees, check result, check attendance, get quick updates from institue, social learning platform, point-of-sale for canteen and all other entities, check bus live location, submit assignments in an organised manner, manage notes, chat app crafted for students needs and many more features that serves all the web requirements of an Institute.

News and Notice Updates

Get relible news and updates of your industry as well as all other domains which is essential for overall development and notices from institute, faculties, class coordinator and all other actors of Intitute.

Smart Canteen POS

Now neither you have to wait in long queues in overcrowded lunch break or wait for your order to get prepared in a 30 Mins lunch break nor canteen owner have to hand the order to unauthentic student. A Smart POS solves all the problem now you can order from POS or Application sitting in the classroom and get notified as the order gets prepared and pay through finemate wallet or All-in-One Card(as we majorly face network issues in institutes situated in countryside)

Smart LMS

A Smart LMS through which you get notified about Submission date, get auto fine reducted and you can also get to know about who has issued the book you need & borrow from him as institutes have 2-3 books per 1000 students.

Marketplace

A Marketplace through which students can buy and sell goods like Artworks, Sketches, Paintings, Used Stationary, Books etc & Services(part time jobs) as well and earn a pocket full of money.
